> ## CHANGE — Moving Outcome Screen: Replace Line-Item Breakdown with Total Cost Display
>
> In `ScreenMovingOutcome.tsx` (Outcome A — Clutter route), replace the current pricing breakdown section entirely.
>
> **Remove:**
> - Monthly storage rate display
> - Separate one-time cost section
> - Progressive discount schedule
> - Labor fee line items

> ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

> **Calculation logic:**
> ```ts
> // Duration in months (from pickup → delivery dates)
> const months = Math.ceil(durationDays / 30)
>
> // Select rate based on duration
> const plan = months >= 8 ? 'longhaul'
>            : months >= 4 ? 'committed'
>            : 'flexible'
>
> const monthlyRate = getBaseRate(sizeIdx, plan)
> const labor = getLaborCost(tier, plan)
>
> const totalCost = (monthlyRate * months)
>                 + labor.pickup
>                 + labor.delivery
>
> // If duration unknown ("Not sure yet"):
> // Show monthly rate only, no total
> ```

> **What's included section** (below the total card — keep this, it's valuable):
> Keep the existing checklist of included services built from their selections. This justifies the total without requiring the customer to do any math themselves.
>
> **If duration is unknown** ("Not sure yet" selected on date screen):
> ```
> $[monthlyRate]/mo

>
> "Set your delivery date anytime from your account
>  portal — we'll calculate your full total then."
